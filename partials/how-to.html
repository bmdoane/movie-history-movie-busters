Welcome to Movie History!

If this is your first time visiting the page, read the instructions below to get started!

  1. Use the search bar to search for new movies to watch.
  2. Once you find a movie, you can add it to your WatchList.
  3. Once you watch a movie, you can give it a rating.
  4. If you give a movie a 10 star rating, you can find it in your favorites.
  5. Click the GO! button below to get started!
